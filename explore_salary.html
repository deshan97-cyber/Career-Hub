Fitter

What's it like to be a Fitter?

Fitters study drawings, plans, specifications and blueprints in order to fit, assemble and shape machinery parts and other equipment. There are different types of Fitters including general Fitters, Mechanical Fitters, Maintenance Fitters, and Fitters and Turners.

Electrician

What's it like to be an Electrician?

Electricians work with electrical systems and networks in domestic, commercial and industrial settings. Electricians are skilled in performing a range of services safely, including installing electrical switchboards, cables and wiring, and diagnosing and repairing electrical malfunctions. The may also perform maintenance on existing electrical systems. The day-to-day tasks of electricians vary depending on their chosen specialisation.

Accountant

What's it like to be an Accountant?

Accountants help individuals and organisations manage their finances, tax credits and obligations, and give advice on compliance and record management. Accountants may choose to specialise and become Management Accountants, Systems Accountants, Fund Accountants or Company Accountants. Accountants may work in the public sector, for large or small organisations, or for individual clients. A bachelor degree or higher is usually required to become an Accountant. Additional certifications may also be needed.
